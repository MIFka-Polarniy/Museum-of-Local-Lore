The Druzhenkov family
Audio guide decoding
The Druzhenkov family significantly contributed to the growth of charitable efforts in the Yaroslavl region. Born in 1850 to the merchant family of Gartsev, Anna Nikolaevna Druzhenkova wedded fellow merchant Dmitry Druzhenkov in 1869. Their company specialized in dealing with fabrics and haberdashery products. The couple dedicated themselves to various charitable causes despite having no children of their own. Their crowning achievement was establishing the Orphanage in 1900. Anna wholeheartedly invested herself in fostering the orphans under her care. Each summer, the young girls were transported to a dacha near Lyutovo Station, located on the left bank of the Shaksha River. Sadly, none of the original buildings exist anymore, with only faint reminders of their foundations remaining.
